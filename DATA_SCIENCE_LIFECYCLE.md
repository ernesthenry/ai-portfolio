# The End-to-End Data Science Lifecycle

This repository is structured to mirror the real-world lifecycle of an AI product, from "Idea" to "Production".

## Phase 1: Problem Identification & Analysis

_Before writing code, we define the math._

- **Regression:** `08_pytorch_fundamentals` (Predicting House Prices)
- **Classification:** `13_ml_algorithms_business` (Churn Prediction)
- **Segmentation:** `13_ml_algorithms_business` (K-Means)

## Phase 2: Data Engineering & Cleaning

_Garbage in, Garbage out._

- **Pipeline:** `11_data_science_pipeline` (Imputation, Scaling, encoding)
- **Processing:** `04_rag_from_scratch` (Chunking and embedding text)

## Phase 3: Model Development (The "Zoo")

_Choosing the right tool for the job._

- **Classical ML:** `14_classical_ml_zoo` (SVM, Isolation Forest, PCA)
- **Deep Learning:** `01_transformer_from_scratch` (Understanding Arhitecture)
- **GenAI:** `07_simple_diffusion` & `02_finetune_llm_qlora`
- **Reinforcement:** `15_reinforcement_learning` (Decision Making)

## Phase 4: Optimization & Engineering

_Making it work better and faster._

- **Patterns:** `05_enterprise_ai_patterns` (Agents, Caching, Routing)
- **Algorithms:** `06_moe_transformer` (Sparse computing)

## Phase 5: MLOps & Deployment

_Keeping it alive in production._

- **Registry & Monitor:** `12_mlops_pipeline` (Drift Detection)
- **Deployment:** `16_deployment_ab_testing` (Docker & FastAPI)
- **Validation:** `16_deployment_ab_testing` (A/B Testing Simulator)

## Phase 6: User Interface

_Delivering value to the user._

- **Frontend:** `10_universal_ui` (Streamlit & Chainlit)
